SURGERYASSIST FAQS

SURGEON HELP

Question 1: How do I register for an account?

1) Go to the homepage login screen at http://surgeryrooms.net/surgeryassist

2) Click the "REGISTER" button.

3) Fill in all required fields with valid information.

4) Click the "REGISTER" button.

5) Email notification is under construction.

Question 2: How do I login to my account?

1) Continue if you have already registered for an account. Otherwise, see Question 1.

2) Go to the homepage login screen at http://surgeryrooms.net/surgeryassist

3) Fill in username and password.

4) Click the "LOGIN" button.

Question 3: What do I do if I forgot my password?

The forgotten password help functionality is currently under construction.

Question 4: How do I go to my account's Home page?

1) Continue if you have already logged in to your account. Otherwise, see Question 2.

2) If the "Home" tab button appears in the top right of the screen you are on, click the "Home" tab.

3) The SurgeryAssist logo icon in the top left corner of every screen is also a clickable button that will take you to your account's Home page.

Question 5: How do I search for ASC room availabilities?

1) Continue if you have already logged in to your account. Otherwise, see Question 2.

2) Go to the Search page by clicking the "Search" tab towards the top right of the screen.

3) Click the "Search for Availabilities" button.

4) Fill out any search criteria you would like to use, then click the "SEARCH" button, or click the "SEE ALL AVAILABILITIES" button to view the entire list of availabilities.

Question 6: How do I request a booking for an ASC room availability?

1) Continue if you have already logged in to your account. Otherwise, see Question 2.

2) Continue if you have already searched for availabilities. Otherwise, see Question 5.

3) Under the "Action" column, click the "SELECT" button that corresponds to the availability you would like to book.

4) Review the availability information. If it is the correct availability that you would like to book, continue. If it is not the correct availability, click the "BACK TO SEARCH" button and repeat step 2.

5) Click the "BOOK AVAILABILITY" button.

6) Enter the required booking details, then click the "PROCEED" button.

7) Review the booking details that you have entered. If you would like to edit any of the details, click the "REVISE" button. If the details are correct, click the "CONFIRM" button.

8) You have now created a pending booking. The corresponding ASC will need to confirm your booking in order for it to become a confirmed booking.

Question 7: How do I view pending booking requests and confirmed bookings?

1) Continue if you have already logged in to your account. Otherwise, see Question 2.

2) Continue if you have already requested a booking for an ASC room availability. Otherwise, see Question 6.

3) Go to the Home page by clicking the "Home" tab towards the top right of the screen.

4) To view confirmed bookings, click on the "Confirmed" sub-tab under "Upcoming Reservations." To view pending booking requests, click on the "Pending" sub-tab under "Upcoming Reservations." The results can be viewed in the table below the sub-tabs.

Question 8: How do I cancel a booking or a booking request?

1) Continue if you have already logged in to your account. Otherwise, see Question 2.

2) Continue if you have already requested a booking for an ASC room availability. Otherwise, see Question 6.

3) Go to the Home page by clicking the "Home" tab towards the top right of the screen.

4) Click on the "Cancel Booking" button.

5) Click on the booking(s) you would like to cancel. To select multiple bookings, hold the CTRL or COMMAND key while clicking. Then click on the "Confirm Cancellations" button.

6) Fill out the "Reason for Cancellation," then click on the "Confirm" button.

7) The booking will be removed from the Upcoming Reservations list.

Question 9: How do I update my profile?

The update profile functionality is currently under construction.

Question 10: How do I logout from my account?

1) Continue if you have already logged in to your account. Otherwise, see Question 2.

2) Click on "Logout" button at the top right of the screen.

Question 5: How do I view room availabilities I have posted?

1) Continue if you have already logged in to your account. Otherwise, see Question 2.

2) Go to the Calendar page by clicking the "Calendar" tab towards the top right of the screen.

3) The availabilities will show up as events on the calendar.

4) The default calendar view is the week view. You can change to month view or day view by using the buttons about the calendar, on the right side. You can view different months, weeks, and days by using the arrow keys above the calendar, on the left side.

Question 6: How do I create room availabilities for surgeons to see?

1) Continue if you have already logged in to your account. Otherwise, see Question 2.

2) Go to the Calendar page by clicking the "Calendar" tab towards the top right of the screen.

3) Click on the calendar where you would like to make an appointment.

4) Fill in the event details. The fields will be automatically filled in with the information from the time/date you clicked on the calendar. When the date and time are correct, click the "Save" button.

5) An event is created on the calendar that represents the availability.

6) You can change the date and time by clicking and dragging the event that is created on the calendar. The end time (A.K.A. the duration of the event) can be changed by clicking and dragging the bottom of the event.

7) To post the availabilities you have created, click the "Save Changes" button at the bottom of the screen.

8) Review the new availabilities information. If it is correct, continue. If it is not correct, click the "Go Back" button and repeat steps 3-7.

9) Click the "Confirm" button.

Question 7: How do I edit room availabilities?

The update profile functionality is currently under construction.

Currently, you cannot edit availabilities once they have been saved/posted. You must delete the availabilities and create new ones to replace them.

Question 8: How do I cancel room availabilities?

1) Continue if you have already logged in to your account. Otherwise, see Question 2.

2) Continue if you have already created room availabilities. Otherwise, see Question 6.

3) Go to the Home page by clicking the "Home" tab towards the top right of the screen.

4) Click the "Cancel Availabilities" button.

5) Click on the availabilities you would like to cancel. To select multiple availabilities, hold the CTRL or COMMAND key while clicking. Then click on the "Confirm Cancellations" button.

6) Fill out the "Reason for Cancellation," then click on the "Confirm" button.

7) The availabilities will be removed from the Calendar and surgeons will no longer be able to view or request them.

Question 9: How do I view surgeons' pending booking requests and confirmed/scheduled bookings?

Question 10: How do I confirm pending booking requests from surgeons?

1) Continue if you have already logged in to your account. Otherwise, see Question 2.

2) Go to the Home page by clicking the "Home" tab towards the top right of the screen.

3) Click the "Accept Bookings" button.

4) Click on the requests you would like to accept. To select multiple requests, hold the CTRL or COMMAND key while clicking. Then click on the "Confirm Bookings" button.

5) Review the booking information, then click on the "Confirm" button.

6) The confirmed bookings will be scheduled and can be viewed under "Upcoming Reservations" on the Home page.
